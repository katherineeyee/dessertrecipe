oat chocolate chip

ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons melted butter
  • ½ cup oil
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg, 1 yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3 cups oats
  • ½ cup chocolate chips

directions

  • preheat oven to 375ºF
  • combine butter, oil, and sugars
  • whisk in egg, yolk, and vanilla
  • add flour, salt, cinnamon, and baking soda
  • fold in oats and chocolate chips
  • scoop dough on baking sheet and flatten
  • bake in oven for 8-12 minutes

claire saffitz's chocolate chip

ingredients

  • 16 tablespoons butter
  • 28 grams milk
  • 150 grams sugar
  • 150 grams brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 2 cold eggs
  • 260 grams flour
  • 6 grams salt
  • 6 grams baking soda
  • 284 grams chocolate chips

directions

  • preheat oven to 350ºF
  • brown 8 tablespoons of butter and pour onto 8 tablespoons of cold butter
  • add milk to butter
  • whisk sugars to butter
  • whisk in vanilla and eggs
  • add flour, salt, and baking soda
  • fold in chocolate chips
  • allow dough to rest for 5 minutes
  • scoop dough and chill for 12-48 hours
  • bake in oven for 18-22 minutes

taylor swift's chai sugar

credit

ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup oil
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon allspice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cloves
  • pinch of black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon baking soda
  • cinnamon sugar for rolling
  • 1 ½ cups powdered sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3 tablespoons milk

directions

  • preheat oven to 350ºF
  • beat butter and oil
  • add sugars and all the spices to butter
  • whisk in vanilla and egg
  • add flour, salt, and baking soda
  • allow dough to rest in fridge for 1 hour
  • scoop dough and flatten
  • roll dough in cinnamon sugar
  • bake in oven for 12-14 minutes
  • combine powdered sugar, cinnamon, and milk to make glaze
  • glaze the cooled cookies

thumbprint

credit

ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ cup jam

directions

  • preheat oven to 350ºF
  • combine butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  • whisk in egg and vanilla
  • add flour, salt, and baking soda
  • chill in plastic wrap for at least an hour
  • roll dough into 1-inch balls on baking sheet
  • press down on each dough ball with thumb
  • fill thumbprint with jam
  • bake in oven for 15 minutes

black seseme oatmeal cookies

credit

ingredients

  • 70 grams roasted black seseme seeds
  • 150 grams sugar
  • 170 grams butter
  • 55 grams brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 125 grams flour
  • ¾ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 205 grams rolled oats

directions

  • preheat oven to 350ºF
  • grind 35 grams of seseme seeds and 50 grams of sugar in food processor until fine and sandy
  • beat butter, seseme mixture, and remaining sugars
  • whisk in egg and vanilla
  • add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
  • gradually combine in oats and remaining seseme seeds
  • scoop dough and flatten
  • bake in oven for 13-16 minutes
