milk bar's chocolate

credit

ingredients

  • 115 grams butter
  • 225 grams sugar
  • 60 grams brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 110 grams buttermilk
  • 105 grams oil
  • 5 grams vanilla
  • 154 grams cake flour
  • 70 grams cocoa powder
  • 6 grams baking powder
  • 7 grams salt

directions

  • preheat oven to 350ºF
  • cream butter and sugars
  • whisk in eggs
  • stream in buttermilk, oil, and vanilla
  • beat mixture until lighter in color and double the size
  • fold in cake flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt
  • pour batter into cake sheet
  • bake in oven for 30-35 minutes

lemon blueberry

credit

ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 250 grams sugar
  • 100 grams brown sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 354 grams flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 240 ml buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 120 ml lemon juice
  • 1 ½ cups blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon flour

directions

  • preheat oven to 350ºF
  • cream butter and sugars
  • whisk in eggs and vanilla
  • fold in flour, baking powder, and salt
  • stream in buttermilk, lemon zest, and lemon juice
  • toss blueberries in flour and fold into the batter
  • pour batter into cake sheet/pan
  • bake in oven for 21-25 minutes

tiramisu

credit

ingredients

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 100 grams sugar
  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 8 oz mascarpone
  • 1 ¾ cups espresso
  • 2 tablespoons rum
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • optional: shaving chocolate

directions

  • whip egg yolks and half of the sugar (50 grams) until light and triple the size
  • whip cream and remaining sugar (50 grams) in separate bowl until soft peaks form
  • add mascarpone and incorporate until medium peaks form
  • fold in both mixtures until fully combined
  • combine espresso and rum in small bowl
  • using sifter, dust cocoa powder in bottom of glass serving dish
  • dip ladyfingers into espresso mixture and place in dish making an even layer
  • spread half of the mascarpone mixture onto ladyfingers in one even layer
  • repeat creating another layer of ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture
  • dust top layer with remaining cocoa powder
  • top with optional chocolate shavings
  • chill in fridge for at least 4 hours

banana bread

credit

ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • optional: ½ cup walnuts or chocolate chips

directions

  • preheat oven to 350ºF
  • melt butter and mash bananas in butter
  • whisk in eggs and vanilla
  • add flour, sugar, salt, baking soda, and cinnamon
  • fold in any optional mix-ins
  • pour batter into loaf pan
  • bake in oven for 45-55 minutes

lemon loaf

credit

ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoon powdered sugar

directions

  • preheat oven to 350ºF
  • combine and set aside flour, baking powder, lemon zest, and salt
  • cream butter and sugar
  • whisk in eggs and vanilla
  • add and mix half of flour mixture
  • add and mix half of buttermilk
  • repeat for remaining flour mixture and buttermilk
  • pour batter into loaf pan
  • bake in oven for 45-55 minutes
