crème brûlée

credit

ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 vanilla bean
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 5 egg yolks
  • ½ cup sugar

directions

  • preheat oven to 325ºF
  • combine cream, vanilla bean, and salt over low heat on stove
  • let sit for a few minutes and discard vanilla bean
  • beat yolks and sugar until light in separate bowl
  • temper the yolk mixture with cream mixture
  • pour into ramekins and place ramekins in baking dish
  • fill baking dish halfway with boiling water
  • bake in oven for 30-40 minutes
  • cool completely and refrigerate for a few hours
  • when ready to serve, top with sugar forming a thin layer
  • blow torch the sugar until golden

chocolate soufflé

ingredients

  • 300 ml milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon flour
  • 300 grams chocolate
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 3 egg whites
  • 5 tablespoons sugar

directions

  • preheat oven to 360ºF
  • butter and sugar ramekins
  • combine milk, butter, vanilla, salt, and flour over low heat on stove
  • add chocolate and egg yolks
  • whip egg whites and sugar until stiff peaks form
  • combine both mixture
  • pour batter into ramekins
  • bake in oven for 20 minutes

cream puffs

credit

ingredients

  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup milk
  • 8 tablspoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

directions

  • preheat oven to 425ºF
  • combine water, milk, butter, sugar, salt over medium heat on stove
  • once boiled, remove from heat and add flour
  • place back over medium heat and stir until thin film forms on bottom of saucepan
  • take off heat and allow dough to cool for a few minutes
  • beat in eggs one at a time until fully incorporated
  • transfer to piping bag and pipe puffs on cooking sheet
  • bake in oven for 10 minutes
  • reduce heat to 325ºF and bake 20-22 additional minutes
  • beat cream, sugar, and vanilla until firm
  • pipe cream into puffs

magnolia bakery's banana pudding

credit

ingredients

  • 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 ½ cups ice cold water
  • 1 package (3.4 oz) instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 box (11 oz) vanilla wafers
  • 6 ripe bananas

directions

  • whisk milk and water until fully homogenous
  • combine pudding mix until smooth
  • cover and refrigerate for 1 hour
  • whip cream until stiff peaks form
  • fold pudding and whipped cream
  • add vanilla and salt
  • in glass serving bowl, spread ¼ of pudding mixture to create first layer
  • place ⅓ of wafers and banana slices to cover the full layer
  • repeat the process ending with one final layer of pudding
  • garnish with wafer pieces and refrigerate for 4-6 hours

claire saffitz's lemon tart

ingredients

  • 40 grams toasted almond flour
  • 130 grams flour
  • 30 grams powdered sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 stick butter, cubed and chilled
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4 teaspoons water
  • 150 grams sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • zest of 2 lemons
  • 1 egg
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 170 grams lemon juice
  • 1 stick butter, cubed and chilled
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 120 grams greek yogurt
  • 100 grams raspberry jam

directions

  • preheat oven to 350ºF
  • add almond flour, flour, sugar, and salt to food processor
  • add butter, yolk, vanilla, and water
  • form tart dough and chill
  • add sugar and salt to a saucepan
  • grate in lemon zest and massage in sugar mixture
  • whisk egg, yolks, and lemon juice
  • bring saucepan to medium heat and whisk until thickened
  • remove from heat and add vanilla and butter cubes one at a time
  • chill lemon curd in fridge
  • mold tart dough into tart shell and parbake with foil for 15-20 minutes
  • add jam to tart crust and bake for 5-7 minutes
  • whisk lemon curd and yogurt
  • spread evenly in tart shell
  • bake in oven for 20-35 minutes or with a slight wobble
  • cool for at least 1 hour

matcha ice cream

credit

ingredients

  • 5 egg yolks
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons matcha powder
  • 1 ½ cups heavy cream

directions

  • whisk yolks and sugar until light pale
  • warm milk, vanilla, and salt over medium heat until it lightly simmers
  • temper the yolk mixture with milk mixture
  • return to medium heat stirring constantly until thickened
  • whisk in matcha powder and cream
  • chill in refridgerator overnight
  • churn the ice cream base in an ice cream maker until the thickness resembles soft serve - around 20 minutes
  • transfer to a container and freeze for at least 4 hours
